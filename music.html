Music

I am known to always have headphones in at all times in my life due to an arguable unhealthy obsession with music. I am fascinated with the production and innate creativity that brings music to life and in some ways it reminds me of what I love about engineering. Just as an engineered product can seem so simple on the outside, there is a whole story of how that product came to be. The same goes for a song and the story that goes into its production and lyrics.

Music often gives us an outlet for emotions that may be difficult to put into words but can easily be represented by a song. A single song has the ability to dictate emotions of ecstasy, grief, or anger that some of us lack the emotional intelligence to put in words. I'm not entirely sure where my life would be without music and for this reason I wanted to give it a special spotlight on my website.
